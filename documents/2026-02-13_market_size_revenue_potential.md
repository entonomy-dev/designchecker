## 1. Methodology and Approach

### 1.1 Market Sizing Framework

This analysis employs a **bottom-up TAM calculation** methodology, considered the most accurate approach for B2B SaaS market sizing. The framework follows this structure:

```
TAM = (Number of Target Customers) × (Average Annual Spend per Customer)

Where:
- Target Customers = Digital marketing agencies in addressable segments
- Annual Spend = (Audits per Year) × (Cost per Audit)
```

### 1.2 Data Sources

**Primary Market Data:**
- IBISWorld industry reports (Digital Advertising Agencies, 2026)
- Agency pricing benchmarks and cost structures (InfluenceFlow, 2026)
- SaaS spending research (Zylo, Backlinko, DemandSage, 2026)
- Competitor pricing analysis (completed February 13, 2026)
- Agency pain points research (completed February 13, 2026)

**Calculation Assumptions:**
All assumptions are documented with sources and conservative estimates applied where ranges exist to ensure realistic projections.

### 1.3 Market Segmentation Approach

The analysis segments the market by:
1. **Company Size:** Solo freelancers, small agencies (2-9 employees), mid-size (10-50), large (50+)
2. **Service Focus:** Design-focused vs. SEO-only vs. full-service digital marketing
3. **Audit Behavior:** Regular audit practices vs. occasional vs. non-auditing agencies
4. **Geography:** US market (primary focus) with global expansion opportunity noted
