## Executive Summary

This document provides detailed profiles of 8 target digital marketing agencies that match DesignChecker's ideal customer profile: agencies with 10-50 employees that regularly conduct web design audits for clients. The research was conducted through web scraping of agency directories (DesignRush, Clutch.co, Digital Agency Network), agency websites, LinkedIn profiles, and business intelligence databases.

**Key Findings:**
- Identified 8 agencies with complete contact information and decision maker details
- Total addressable market: 589+ web design agencies in USA (per Semrush)
- Average project budget: $10,000-$25,000
- Typical audit pricing: $1,500-$3,000 per audit
- 63% of businesses outsource work to agencies (2026 data)

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

Based on this research, the ideal DesignChecker customer is:

### Company Characteristics
- **Size:** 10-50 employees
- **Type:** Digital marketing agency or web design studio
- **Services:** Web design, digital marketing, SEO, branding
- **Location:** United States (initial focus)
- **Age:** 5+ years in business (established processes)

### Current Behavior
- Regularly conducts design audits or reviews for clients
- Charges $10,000-$25,000 per project
- Hourly rates $100-$200
- Has 20+ active clients annually
- Struggles with manual audit processes

### Decision Maker Profile
- **Title:** CEO, Founder, Creative Director, Operations Director
- **Mindset:** ROI-focused, data-driven, efficiency-oriented
- **Values:** Client satisfaction, competitive differentiation, scalability
- **Pain Points:**
  - Time spent on manual design evaluation
  - Difficulty justifying design decisions objectively
  - Inconsistent quality across projects
  - Client education on design value

### Buying Signals
- Already offering design audit or UX audit services
- Using terms like "design evaluation," "website audit," "UX assessment"
- Mentioning data-driven approach or ROI focus in marketing
- Recent growth or multiple locations (scaling challenges)
- Premium pricing ($150+/hour suggests budget for tools)
